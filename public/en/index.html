Process

Process

A clear website starts with a clear plan. We guide the project step by step, so you always know what we are doing, why it matters and what happens next.

01

Brief

We learn about your business, goals, audience, offer and timeline. This helps us understand what the website should achieve before we start designing.

02

Structure

We plan the page structure, key sections, navigation and contact paths, so visitors can quickly understand your offer and take action.

03

Build

We create a responsive website, connect the contact form, prepare the content layout and check speed, usability and mobile experience.

04

Launch

We prepare the website for publication, basic SEO, indexing and future improvements, so it is ready to grow with your business.

FAQ

Short answers before we talk

How much does a website cost?

The price depends on pages, languages, integrations and content. After a short brief, you receive a clear scope and quote.

Landing page or business website?

A landing page fits one service or campaign. A business website is better when several parts of the offer need space.

Will the website be SEO-ready?

Yes. Basic tags, headings, URL structure, speed and sitemap are prepared from the start.

Does the contact form work?

Yes. The form sends data to the Cloudflare Pages Function at /api/contact and keeps CORS and Turnstile protection.
