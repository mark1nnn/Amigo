How to prepare a website for Google?

SEO for a small business website starts with fundamentals, not tricks. Clear structure, useful content and good technical hygiene make it easier for both users and search engines to understand the page.

June 9, 20265 min read

Start with user questions

Headings and sections should answer real client questions about services, process, location or scope. That makes the page easier to scan and gives it clearer topical signals.

Use a clean heading structure and metadata

Each page should have one H1, logical H2 sections and unique title and description tags. The goal is clarity, not keyword stuffing.

Do not ignore technical basics

  • responsive layout,
  • reasonable loading speed,
  • internal links between relevant pages,
  • sitemap and readable URLs,
  • images with meaningful names and alt text.

SEO works best with a strong offer

Even technically correct pages underperform if the offer is vague. SEO works best together with clear service pages, a useful contact form and strong internal linking to services, portfolio and contact.
