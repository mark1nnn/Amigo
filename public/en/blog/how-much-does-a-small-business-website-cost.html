How much does a small business website cost?

Website pricing is rarely just one number. It usually depends on the scope, the number of pages, the quality of the source materials and whether the site only needs to look good or also support inquiry flow and future growth.

June 9, 20264 min read

What affects the budget most?

The biggest factor is scope. A simple landing page is faster to design and build than a company website with several subpages, tailored forms and a more detailed content structure.

  • number of sections and pages,
  • language versions,
  • content preparation and editing,
  • forms, maps and supporting blocks,
  • room for future development.

Why can two websites cost very differently?

Two businesses may both ask for “a website” and still need completely different work. One may already have copy, visuals and a clear offer. The other may first need help structuring services and deciding what should appear on the page at all.

What should you prepare before asking for pricing?

Clear goals always help. It is useful to define whether you need one page or several, which services matter most, whether the site needs more than one language and what kind of information the contact form should collect.
