About Amigo

Amigo is a small web studio helping small businesses launch clear, fast and practical websites.

Simple process

We start with the website goal, audience and contact path, then shape the structure and interface.

Clear communication

Scope, price and timeline should be clear before the work starts. Projects move better when priorities are known.

SEO-ready base

Headings, descriptions, URLs, forms and performance are planned early, not patched at the end.

Support after launch

After publication, the website can grow with new sections, pages, language versions, FAQ and blog content.

Honest positioning

Honest positioning

We do not describe Amigo as a large agency or add unconfirmed client results. The website should clearly show how we can help and what is needed to estimate the project.
