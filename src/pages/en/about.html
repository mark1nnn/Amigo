About

Who is behind Amigo?

Amigo is a small web studio creating fast, clear and responsive websites for small businesses. We help turn a business offer into a website that presents services clearly, makes contact easier and can grow over time.

About Amigo

A small studio with a practical approach

At Amigo, we focus on simple and effective solutions: landing pages, company websites, contact forms, basic SEO structure and support after launch. We do not add complexity for its own sake. First, we define the website goal, the most important information for the visitor and the best contact path.

A good website does not need to be overloaded with effects. It should quickly explain what the business does, which services it offers and what the user can do next: call, send a form, view the portfolio or come back to the offer later.

Principles

What matters to us

Clear communication

Scope, timeline and cost should be understandable before work begins.

Clear offer

The website should help a visitor quickly understand what the business offers and how to get in touch.

Responsiveness

The project must work comfortably on phones, tablets and desktop screens.

Honest expectations

We do not add unverified results, reviews or statistics. We prefer to build trust through real work and a clear process.

Positioning

No empty promises

We do not describe Amigo as a large agency and we do not promise results that cannot be verified. A website can help with presenting an offer, improving contact, supporting advertising and building search visibility, but its effectiveness also depends on content, market, offer quality, traffic and the work done after launch.

Our goal is to prepare a strong base: a clear structure, a responsive layout, a working contact form, basic SEO and a website that can grow with the business.
